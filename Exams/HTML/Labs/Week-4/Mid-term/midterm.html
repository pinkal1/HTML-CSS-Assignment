Couple of months ago, I was on a trip to Hawaiian island for a vacation. Me, along with my friends, on our way back were struct in the Island because of bad weather and were forced to stay back in the Island. That's when the simple things, yet very effective, which I carried along with me helped us alot during our stay in the Island.

Portable Knife Set:

Portable Knife Set can be useful in the stranded places like Island. It can be used as hunting tool and also, when it required it can be used as a self defence tool.

Net Fishing:

In situations like this, Fishing Net can be quite useful. Fishing Net offers an easy solution to dealing with scrapes from skinned and gutted fish and is an ideal way to maximize your time and effort while out on the water.

Matchboxes:

Matchboxes helps in great deal while we are stranded in the resourceless localities. Helps us in lighting the fire, also helps in cooking the food.bug spray!

Hawaiian Island

The Hawaiian island of Maui is a destination for true nature lovers. The ethereal bamboo forest and churning waterfalls in Haleakala National Park can be explored via the Waimoku Falls Trail.

Where is it located?:
Hawaiian Islands located about 3,200 kilometers (2,000 miles) from the continental United States.
What can you find?
Hawaiian Islands are archipelago of eight major islands, several atolls, numerous smaller islets, and undersea seamounts.
How do you plan to survive?
Surviving on Hawaiian Islands has become a rather easy job these days given that the number of visitors are increasing day-by-day. Also, in-case of any emergency, it's all suggested to carry the above mentioend items.
