Granny's Fusion - Restaurant & Catering.

Granny's Fusion has been in the business for over 36 years. we take pride in our food, service and courteousness. At Granny's, we believe in the power of food that is handmade.

A modern tavern nestled in a bucolic Central Park setting, Granny's Fusion is an iconic, landmark restaurant unlike any other.

Centered around a hearty, rustic menu shaped by local, seasonal ingredients, Granny's Fusion invites patrons into a warm, celebratory gathering space that captures the spirit of Central Park and the energy of San Jose locality.

Originally designed by Calvert Vaux and built in 1870 to house the sheep that grazed in Central Park's Sheep Meadow, Granny's Fusion has proudly served locals, presidents, royalty, artists, actors, and first-time visitors since 1934.
