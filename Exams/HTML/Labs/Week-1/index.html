Definition of Hobby:

Hobby is something a person acquires it from childhood irrespective of the age. We all do some kind of work according to our interest which can give us happiness and joy that is called hobby. Some people get different hobbies according to their interest, likes and dislikes.

Our hobbies help us in earning live hood and make a successful career. Hobby is something we can fully enjoy in our leisure or free time.
