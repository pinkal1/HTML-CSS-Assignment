Description of Latest Smart Phones

Google Nexus 6P     HTC One M9     Apple iPhone 6S

Google Nexus 6P:

1. Considerably thinner: Nexus 6 arrived with a rather clunky, 0.4 inches-thick body, which precluded it from being tagged as elegant-looking.
2. New color options: Nexus 6P comes in three, instead of two, colors. "Frosted white", "Aluminum", and "Graphite".
3. Smaller screen: 6-inch Nexus 6P came as a medium-sized shock, given how Google was, until then, content with 5- or sub-5" devices.
4. Larger battery: For Nexus 6P, Google has improved its Nexus phablet with a larger, 3,450 mAh cell (vs 3,220 mAh with the Nexus 6).

HTC One M9:

1. Display: 5.0 inch, Full HD 1080p; Corning Gorilla Glass.
2. Main Camera: 20MP with sapphire cover lens, auto-focus, BSI sensor, f/2.2, 27.8mm lens, 4K video recording.
3. Front camera: HTC UltraPixel, BSI sensor, f/2.0, 26.8mm lens, 1080p video recording.
4. Battery: 2840 mAh; Talk time: Up to 25.4 hours for 2G/ 21.7 hours for 3G; Standby time: Up to 391 hours for 2G/ 402 hours for 3G.

Apple iPhone 6S:

Apple iPhone 6S has following features:

1. Processor: A9 chip with 64-bit architecture and embedded M9 motion coprocessor.
2. Camera: 12-megapixel with 1.22 pixels iSight camera with Autofocus, Focus Pixels and True Tone flash.
3. M9 Motion Compressor: Provides a wide range of fitness tracking capabilities to help you measure how much you do every day.
4. Video: FaceTime with HD camera for 5MP photos (1280x960) and 720p HD video recording. Backside illumination sensor.
