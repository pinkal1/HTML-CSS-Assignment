    Knife

    A cutting instrument consisting of a sharp-edged often pointed blade of metal fitted into a handle or onto a machine. As I am on adventure tour, I always carry some set of tools. One among them is knife.

    Rope

    I have a synthetic nylon rope that is about 250 meters long. I carry this item generally for trekking small hills.

    Lighter

    A device that produces a small flame. I carry this as most time we go with camp fire. People sometimes forget to bring the match sticks. So, I always carry one.

    Hover Island

I can survive for couple of days by fishing and drinking the lake water. Fortunately, it has some trees such as coconut tree, banana tree and some small fruit trees. It has lots of timber tree.
