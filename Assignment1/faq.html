Frequently asked questions

  • Do I have to schedule a party? Can I just come in and use a table to throw my own party?
    Yes, you must schedule a party inside Pixieland. Walk-in parties are not permitted (bringing in a cake, balloons and/or gifts is considered a party).

  • Can I pay with a check?
    No, we do not accept checks. We do accept cash, visa, mastercard and discover. The balance is due prior to the start of your party.

  • How much is the deposit? The Mini and Ultimate Pixie Party Packages are $100.00 non-refundable deposits (applies to the balance). The Windy Grove Private Party Package and the Pixie Private Party Package is a $200.00 non-refundable deposit
